About Us

Allison M.

Hi guys! My name is Allison and I was born and raised in San Fransisco. We wanted to make a website to help people with resources to get help with food. I think it's important for there to be more resources like these in San Francisco because its an important topic that isnt talked about enough. I've seen families who have struggled that could have used these valuable resources.

Ngoc T.

Hello, I'm Ngoc! I was born in Vietnam and raised in San Francisco. In San Francisco, one of the most expensive cities to live in the US, income disparity is big issue. Everyone deserves to have access to the resources and necessities to live comfortably. With that in mind, my team and I created a website with many resources for both people in need of food and for people who want help us with our mission.

William H.
